时光的独白 | Monologue of Time

在这个阳光洒满的瞬间，童年的我像一朵盛开的花，纯真而又无畏。每一个笑容背后都有无数的梦，像是星星般闪烁。岁月在流转，但那些快乐的时光依旧在心中温暖如初。希望在未来的旅程中，依然能保持这份简单的幸福。


In this moment bathed in sunshine, the child version of me is like a blooming flower, innocent and fearless. Behind every smile lie countless dreams, twinkling like stars. As time flows on, those joyful moments remain warm in my heart. I hope to carry this simple happiness with me on my journey into the future.

童年记忆 | Childhood Memories

这是我童年的一瞬，脸上挂着阳光般的笑容，眼中闪烁着对世界的好奇。每一张单人照片，都是一段故事的开端。无忧无虑的日子，像一幅五彩斑斓的画卷，承载着无尽的梦想与期待。愿这份纯真永驻心间，成为我前行的动力。


This is a moment from my childhood, with a sunshine-like smile on my face and curiosity sparkling in my eyes. Each solo photo is the beginning of a story. Those carefree days are like a colorful canvas, filled with endless dreams and expectations. May this innocence remain in my heart forever, becoming the driving force for my journey ahead.

无私的爱 | Selfless Love

每当我遇到困难，外婆总是用她那双布满岁月痕迹的手，轻轻握住我的手，告诉我：没关系，就算全世界都质疑你， 我永远是你最坚强的后盾， 专注去做你想做的事，坚定自己的选择， 一定要开开心心的最喜欢的事， 希望我永远都不要有烦恼， 只要你想要的 都会有的。


Whenever I encountered difficulties, my grandmother would always hold my hands gently with her hands full of traces of time and tell me: It doesn’t matter, even if the whole world doubts you, I will always be your strongest supporter, focus on doing what you want to do, be firm in your choice, and be happy to do what you like most. I hope I will never have any worries, and I will have everything you want.
